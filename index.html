Welcome to my quote attribution survey! Your job is to select the quote/person that best matches the given person/quote. It is possible that the person-quote combination you choose is not historically accurate, that is understandable. Just follow your gut about what feels right.

The only information this website collects is which radio button you select, and I do not keep track of any personal information that might be accesible by a browser.

If you suspect that the survey is not working correctly, feel free to shoot me an email at ordelore (at) uchicago (dot) edu.

How to take this survey:

  1. Read the provided name or quote.
  2. Choose the quote or name that best matches the provided name or quote.
  3. Press 'Submit' to send your response and generate a new prompt.
